Click and a CompoundPolyShape will begin forming at that point!
BACKSPACE / DELETE will clear any currently built shapes.
SPACE will pause the rotation of any pre-existing shapes.

CAMERA:
Mouse left-drag: Rotate the camera around the subject
Mouse right drag: will zoom in and out
Mouse middle-drag (command-left-drag on mac): pan
Mouse double-click: restores the camera to its original position
SHIFT: constrains rotation and panning to one axis or the other

COLOR:
1-0 on the keyboard are hotkey'd to intervals of hues.
left or right on the arrow keys will further adjust hue.
